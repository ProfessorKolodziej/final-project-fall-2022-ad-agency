About the Database

Advertising lives in the greater funnel of marketing as one of its most creative functionalities. There are thousands of agencies in advertising and many agencies overlap from marketing. As a result, the advertising industry is constantly changing with new forms of specializations and capabilities, so the lists are ever-growing.

AdBase (as of December 2022) is a database designed to filter top advertising agencies across industries, specialties, and demographics based in the US. We researched agencies from top grossing to award winning to provide a diverse representation of the industry.

AdBase was created by aspiring agency professionals sifting through Google, blogs, and ‘top lists’ to create a hot list of agencies in pursuit of internships and jobs. We struggled to find a single platform that categorized an encompassing sampling of advertising agencies in the US. And so, we came up with AdBase. We wish you the best of luck in your search!

*This database was sorted by undergraduate seniors supported by professors and professionals.*
